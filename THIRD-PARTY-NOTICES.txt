.NET Runtime collation tailoring
--------------------------------

The native Word Frequency current-culture comparer adapts the ICU width and
kana tailoring tables and rules from dotnet/runtime's
System.Globalization.Native pal_collation.c.

PCRE2 10.47 (PCRE2-16 static library)
-------------------------------------

The native C++ regular-expression search and builder use PCRE2-16, built
through the pinned vcpkg manifest. WinForge does not use PCRE2 JIT.
