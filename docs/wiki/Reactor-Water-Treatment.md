# Reactor Water Treatment · 反應堆水處理

**EN —** The reactor depends on an ultra-realistic makeup-water plant that turns raw intake water into ultrapure reactor-grade water through a full treatment train, tracking real chemistry parameters along the way. When the makeup tank empties or chemistry drifts, the reactor degrades — so the water plant is a real dependency, not decoration.

**粵語 —** 反應堆依賴一個超寫實補給水廠，將原水經過完整處理流程變成超純反應堆級水，沿途追蹤真實水質參數。當補給水箱耗盡或水質漂移，反應堆會劣化——所以水廠係真實依賴，唔係裝飾。

---

## The treatment train · 處理流程

**EN —** Raw water flows through the full chain before it is fit for the reactor:

**粵語 —** 原水喺適合反應堆使用之前，要流過完整鏈條：

**Intake → Clarifier → Filter → RO (Reverse Osmosis) → Demineralizer → Degasifier → Ultrapure storage**

**取水 → 澄清器 → 過濾器 → RO（逆滲透）→ 除鹽器 → 除氣器 → 超純水儲存**

| Stage · 階段 | Purpose · 用途 |
|---|---|
| **Intake · 取水** | Draw raw feed water. · 抽取原水。 |
| **Clarifier · 澄清器** | Settle out suspended solids. · 沉降懸浮固體。 |
| **Filter · 過濾器** | Remove fine particulates. · 移除細微顆粒。 |
| **RO · 逆滲透** | Reject dissolved salts across a membrane. · 經薄膜去除溶解鹽。 |
| **Demineralizer · 除鹽器** | Ion-exchange to ultra-low ionic content. · 離子交換至超低離子含量。 |
| **Degasifier · 除氣器** | Strip dissolved O₂ and CO₂. · 去除溶解 O₂ 同 CO₂。 |
| **Ultrapure storage · 超純水儲存** | Hold reactor-grade makeup water. · 儲存反應堆級補給水。 |

---

## Chemistry · 水質

**EN —** The plant drives the water to reactor-grade spec, tracking:

- **Conductivity · 電導率** — driven down to ultrapure (e.g. 45 → **0.055 µS/cm**).
- **pH** — held in the controlled band.
- **Dissolved O₂ · 溶解氧** — stripped to a few ppb (e.g. → 5 ppb).
- **Silica · 二氧化矽** and **chlorides · 氯化物** — kept to trace levels to protect the core.

**粵語 —** 水廠將水帶到反應堆級規格，追蹤：

- **電導率** — 降至超純（例如 45 → **0.055 µS/cm**）。
- **pH** — 保持喺受控範圍。
- **溶解氧** — 去除至幾個 ppb（例如 → 5 ppb）。
- **二氧化矽**同**氯化物** — 保持微量水平以保護爐心。

---

## How the reactor depends on it · 反應堆點樣依賴它

**EN —** Makeup water feeds the primary inventory. As the ultrapure tank fills it sustains availability; when the tank empties, **makeup availability degrades** (e.g. 1.00 → 0.02) and a **low-tank alarm** raises. Shutting the supply valve drops availability to 0. Bad chemistry or a starved tank propagate into the reactor as degraded inventory and alarms.

**粵語 —** 補給水供應一次側存量。當超純水箱注滿，會維持可用率；當水箱耗盡，**補給可用率會劣化**（例如 1.00 → 0.02）並引發**低水位警報**。關閉供應閥會令可用率跌至 0。劣質水質或耗盡嘅水箱會傳遞到反應堆，造成存量劣化同警報。
